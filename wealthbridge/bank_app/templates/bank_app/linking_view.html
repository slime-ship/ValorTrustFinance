Dear Valued Customer,

We are pleased to inform you about an important update to our banking services. To enhance the security and ease of your transactions, we are introducing a new account activation PIN process.

How It Works

To activate your account and enable purchases, you will need to use an activation PIN. This PIN is essential for verifying your account and ensuring secure transactions. Please follow these simple steps:

  1. Log in to our website or mobile app.
  2. Navigate to the Account Activation section.
  3. Enter the activation PIN provided to you.
  4. Complete the activation process to start making purchases.

If you haven't received your activation PIN, please contact our customer support team to request it. We are here to assist you 24/7 with any questions or issues you may have.
